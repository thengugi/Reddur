One night with the Jass b'stards

English | 20 mins | Year of production 2014 |

'One night with The Jass B'stards' features notorious Delhi based jazz-punk act The Jass B'stards. Wanphrang K Diengdoh follows the group in some of their weirdest performances.

Reviews

Delhi based trio The Jass B’stards have recently released a mockumentary titled ‘One Night With The Jass B’stards’. The short film has been directed, filmed and edited by the very talented Wanphrang Diengdoh.....

One Night With The Jass B’stards’, directed by Shillong/Delhi-based filmmaker Wanphrang Diengdoh, captures the three-member band at their funniest and grooviest best....
